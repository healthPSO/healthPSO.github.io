Launching Event


April 16, 2018 marked the launch of the Policy Support Observatory, the product of collaboration between the Ministry of Public Health, the World Health Organization and the American University of Beirut. The Deputy Prime Minister and Health Minister Ghassan Hasbani signed the agreement on the launch of this Observatory with the acting Director of the WHO-EMR Dr. Jawad Al Mahjour, AUB President Fadlo Khuri, in the presence of Dr. Atef Majdalani, the Director General of the Ministry Dr. Walid Ammar, the WHO representative in Lebanon Gabriel Riedner, the Dean of the Faculty of Health Sciences at the AUB Dr. Iman Nouwayhad, heads of syndicates and representatives from different organizations.

Dr. Walid Ammar noted that the Policy Support Observatory aimed at consolidating the approach of good governance practices adopted by the Ministry, in order to enhance the same and ensure the continuity of the accomplishments achieved by the Healthcare sector so far. The distinctive participation of the Director-General of the WHO, Dr. Tedros Adhanom Ghebereysus, reflected the importance of the initiative, not only for Lebanon but also for the global public health community.
