National Health Forum

A core task of the PSO is to provide the permanent secretariat for the organisation of National Health Forum events. The National Health Forum (NHF) consists of two activities; a periodical NHF Conference that occurs at least once a year, and a platform of Communities of Practices. These events involve a wide range of stakeholders who convene in ongoing participative debates around key health sector issues. The PSO assists the Organising Committee of the Conference in preparing, implementing and following-up on these events.

Objectives:

  • Increased visibility of the technical work, achievements and innovations produced by health sector actors;
  • Heightened awareness among stakeholders about competing priorities and emerging problems;
  • Receptivity of public opinion to rational policies.
Stakeholders: Ministries, parliamentarians, governmental bodies, policy-makers, academia, UN agencies, media, NGOs, field actors and others.

The First NHF Conference was planned for December 2019 and the preparations kicked-start in the third PSO guiding committee meeting as members agreed on its theme and format. Due to the unrest that was happening in the country and then the expanding pandemic, this conference had to be put on hold.
